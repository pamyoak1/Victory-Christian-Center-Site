Our Mission Statement


Build a firm foundation for the new life

Matthew 28:19-20 
Jesus himself gave the church her great mission. To victory Christian Center this means we must impact this community with a Godly influence.
We will accomplish this in four ways:


Win The Lost
Since the lost people matter to God, they must also matter to us. This must be our first priority
Minister To The Hurting
Most people are hurting because of broken trusts and broken relationships. We want to connect people to the One who can heal those broken relationships and restore their trust in God
Develop Strong Church Families
Beginning with children's ministries, we will endeavor to install Biblical principles into every church family. By developing strong families, we will develop a strong church
Develop Principles
Teach people to teach others by spending time in the word, in prayer, and getting closer to God.
