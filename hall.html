Hall Information and Rental



Victory Christian Center has a hall that is available for rent for any occassion! The hall is equipped with dozen of long tables and chairs, cooling and heating as well. Bathrooms and kid activities such as pool table, skyball, and ping pong table are also available at the hall. There are no appliances available except for a fridgerator.

If you have a birthday party, anniversary, wedding reception or any other occassion that you need a hall to accomidate a group of people, the hall is a good place to rent. For more information please go to our contact page or call us at: (740) 344-8894
