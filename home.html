Welcome!!!

Victory Christian Center building has been around since the 1970's and was newly named Victory Christian Center Church of God in the summer of 2015. Victory Christian Center was established in 1958 and is now one of the newly established churches in Newark, Ohio. The church has been newly restructured from the inside with a new atmosphere and a differnet outlook than previous years.
